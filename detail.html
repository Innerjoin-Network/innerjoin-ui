We find ourselves at a key moment in history. A global pandemic. Social isolation. Self-quarantine. People who met to worship Jesus together, now unable to do so.

A key quote from the book of Acts: "Now the entire church, which had gathered together to hear Peter speak, went out to him with one accord."

A key quote from the Book of Acts: "I shall never see another man like you."

If we allow Christianity to be abandoned or denied in the West, we deny the message of the Gospel to those in most need. We may be stopping a virus from spreading. The metaphor of bacterial invasion does well to capture the moral urgency and fundamental imperative for our leaders in the West.
